Autonomous UVC Disinfection Robot

The Challenge: In response to the global pandemic's urgent need for safe, pathogen-free environments, the primary challenge was to create a disinfection solution that was not only effective but fully autonomous.

My Leadership & Action: I led the entire product lifecycle, from initial concept to final integration. I personally developed the 2D/3D layouts in SolidWorks, created simulations in 3DS MAX, and handled the initial robot programming. Leading a team of one engineer and three technicians, I managed the project timeline and budget, coordinated with suppliers, and conducted field tests, ultimately securing client approval.

Key Technical Solution: A critical challenge was balancing the total weight, as the powerful UVC emitters required heavy batteries. A robust safety system, integrating multiple sensors, was engineered to ensure the environment was completely clear of personnel during disinfection cycles, a non-negotiable safety premise.
